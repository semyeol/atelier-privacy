Atelier Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Atelier is a personal wardrobe app: you photograph your clothing, organize the pieces, and compose outfits. This policy explains what information the app handles, where it goes, and what never leaves your device.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and, if you share it, your name. We use this solely to create and authenticate your account.
  • Your content. The clothing images you save, and the details you attach to them (name, type, color, brand, size, favorites), plus the outfits ("fits") you compose. This content is private to your account.
  • Service counters. We keep a daily count of your AI identification requests to enforce fair-use limits.
  • Location (optional). If you allow location access, your approximate location is used only to fetch the local weather (via Apple WeatherKit) so outfit suggestions match the day. It is used in the moment, never stored, and never shared. The app works fully without it.

How your photos are handled

  • Background removal happens entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Your original photo — including anything in the background — is never uploaded and never leaves your phone.
  • Only the cropped image of the clothing item itself is stored with your account so it can sync and appear in your wardrobe.
  • If AI clothing detection is enabled (you can turn it off in Settings), a small copy of the cropped item image is sent to Google's Gemini API to suggest the item's type, color, name, and brand. The image is used only to produce that suggestion; we do not use it for advertising or to train our own models. Google's processing is governed by its Gemini API terms.

Where your data is stored

Your account, content, and images are stored with Supabase, our infrastructure provider. Images live in private storage that only your authenticated account can access, enforced at the database level.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No selling or sharing of your data with data brokers.
  • No tracking across other apps or websites.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual pieces and fits at any time, which permanently removes them and their images. You can delete your entire account in Settings → Delete Account: this permanently erases your account, all saved pieces, images, fits, and usage counters. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Children

Atelier is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date above. Material changes will be noted in the app.
